Objectives

SEF objective focuses on the quality and relevance of skills & Employment in keeping with national and global context.

√ To develop skills for employment

√ To conduct demand driven TVET

√ To identify skills gap

√ To identify skills mismatch

√ To conduct research on employment opportunities at home and abroad

√ To identify demand on skills in the local and overseas market.

SEF Mission & Vission

Mission
1. To contribute in national economy through developing Technically skilled Human Resources.
2. Development of Employability Skills and Creation of Entrepreneurs.

Vission
To be a recognized research center in the national and international arena for contributing to the development of skills and Employment for the world of work.
